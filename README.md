## The Philosophy

Most AI tools give you a chatbot and call it a day. Journey AI is different.

We deploy **fleets of specialized AI agents** — each an expert in its domain — that debate, validate, and cross-check each other before making a single recommendation. No hallucination-prone single-model answers. No black boxes. Just transparent, multi-perspective intelligence that explains *why* it thinks what it thinks.

> **One agent is a tool. Twenty agents working together is an intelligence.**

### <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-00C853?style=flat-square" width="12"> Stock Advisor — *Wall Street Intelligence. On Your Laptop.*

> **27-signal Bayesian fusion engine** + multi-agent AI debate = institutional-grade trading intelligence for everyone.

Hedge funds spend millions on quantitative analysis. Stock Advisor brings the same analytical depth to your personal machine — fully offline, fully transparent, fully yours.

| What the System Does | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Fuses 27 signal sources** | Technical, fundamental, sentiment, ML predictions, macro, insider flow, options data — unified through 6-phase Bayesian log-odds fusion |
| **AI agents debate every trade** | Bull vs. Bear vs. Fundamental agents argue in structured rounds; a Synthesizer resolves disagreements |
| **Pre-market intelligence daily** | Automated 9:10 AM pipeline scans the entire market, filters through risk gates, delivers top picks |
| **14 strategies compete live** | Paper trading arena with genetic algorithm capital allocation — strategies evolve or die |
| **Self-learning system** | Signal weights auto-optimize weekly via genetic algorithms + Bayesian changepoint detection |
| **Teaches you to trade** | AI financial tutor with 63 concepts, quizzes using your real portfolio, progressive difficulty |

**The Stack**: Python + Claude Opus (multi-agent debate) + XGBoost/LightGBM + FinBERT NLP + Telegram bot + FastAPI

**Risk Management**: Kelly Criterion sizing, CVaR tail risk, drawdown circuit breakers, regime-aware position gates.
